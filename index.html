About Me

I am a senior MLE at Xpeng Motors, based in San Diego, CA. I am currently working on camera perception for autonomous driving, focusing on BEV map learning. Previously, I graduated from the University of South Carolina with a PhD in Computer Science in 2022. My research fields included semantic segmentation, image matching, few-shot learning, and homography estimation.
